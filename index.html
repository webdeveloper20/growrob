About

GrowRob was formed as an idea on February 20, 2022. The reason I created the device was based on a big problem in the rural economy. Currently, water shortage and air pollution will continue for the next 10 years. There is a 33% loss in agriculture, which is a big indicator. We thought and found the solution to the problems in our own device. Currently, there is almost no such device as growrob in the world market. But our research continues. Investment must be made to create the device. Our unit reached the final stage of the Teknofest Azerbaijan competition in the field of Agro technology with 93.3 points out of 100 points.
